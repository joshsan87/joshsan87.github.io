Starset - Silos

Silos (stylized as SILOS) is the fifth studio album created by Starset, which released on September 12, 2025. It features a total of ten songs and six interludes for a total of 16 tracks. Eight of these songs were released as singles prior to the album's release. These singles were "Brave New World," "Degenerate," "TokSik," "Dystopia," "Dark Things," "Head Over Heels," "Silos," and "Shattered Dreams."

Sleep Token - Even in Arcadia

Even in Arcadia is the fourth studio album by anonymous English rock band Sleep Token. It was released on 9 May 2025 by RCA Records.[1] The album garnered significant media attention amid an unorthodox marketing campaign that has included a weatherman at WRAL in North Carolina providing hints to release dates and album singles.[2]
